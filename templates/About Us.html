Blockchain and Machine Learning Solution for building Trust in Textiles

ABOUT US

A robust system for predicting fabric quality, leveraging advanced technologies to enhance accuracy and efficiency in the dynamic textile industry.
Integration of blockchain-based supply chain boosts transparency, revolutionizing fabric quality assurance and meeting industry needs.
This aims to assess fabric quality before the consumer purchases the product.
We use the Ethereum platform, an open-source decentralized blockchain, which securely runs and verifies smart contracts on a peer-to-peer network.
Cryptographic hashing methods generate a unique hash ID for each fabric batch.
After purchasing the fabric, the customer can track its supply chain using the unique ID on the label, ensuring authenticity and ethical sourcing.
MetaMask is a cryptographic digital wallet for managing and interacting with the Ethereum blockchain.
It allows users to store, send, and receive Ethereum tokens, while also providing an easy-to-use interface for accessing decentralized finance (DeFi) platforms.
Ganache is ideal for testing and development, ensuring seamless integration of smart contracts for fabric traceability and quality verification.
